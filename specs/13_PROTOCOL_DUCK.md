# Duck Protocol

**Status:** Spec v2 (rewritten 2026-05-03)
**Species code:** `duck`
**Sub-types:** `duck_type = 'meat' | 'layer'` (chosen at batch creation — see `02_BATCH_MANAGEMENT.md` §4 wizard Step 1b)
**Lifecycle:**
- `meat`: 8–10 weeks (default 8)
- `layer`: 72+ weeks (default 78; egg production starts Week 20+ per CONVENTIONS §2.7)
**Traditional remedies:** YES — culturally appropriate for West African smallholder duck farming; all five remedies below are research-validated (see §7).

---

## 1. Overview

Ducks are raised in both commercial (intensive) and mixed/smallholder (semi-intensive) systems across Ghana and Nigeria. This protocol drives the seed configuration for `species_config` rows where `species = 'duck'`, branched by `duck_type`.

Key duck-specific facts that drive the protocol:

1.1. Ducks consume **1.5–2× the water** of chickens at the same age (head-submersion behaviour, higher metabolism). See §2.
1.2. **Niacin deficiency causes leg deformities.** Niacin is delivered in **drinking water**, not feed (CONVENTIONS §2.9). The Water-Health module auto-generates a niacin task for every duck batch from Day 1. Schedule documented in §4.0; full implementation lives in `03_WATER_HEALTH.md` §8.
1.3. **Duck Viral Hepatitis (DVH)** is the #1 first-week killer. Vaccine is **subcutaneous injection** (`delivery_method = 'injection_subcutaneous'`), not water-based. UI handling in §10.
1.4. Coccidiosis pressure is **lower than in chickens** — protocol uses single-week prevention rather than continuous coccidiostat.
1.5. Duck diseases (DVH, Duck Plague) are **distinct** from chicken diseases; vaccination set differs.
1.6. Layer ducks (`duck_type = 'layer'`) follow the meat schedule for Weeks 1–8, then transition to a maintenance schedule and begin egg production tracking at Week 20+ (see `06_EGG_PRODUCTION.md`).

## 3. Disease Profile

| Code | Disease | Risk | Window | Prevention path |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| D1 | Duck Viral Hepatitis | CRITICAL | Day 1–14 | DVH vaccine Day 7 (sub-cut) |
| D2 | Duck Plague (Anatid herpesvirus) | HIGH (endemic regions) | Day 14+ | Duck Plague vaccine Day 14, booster Day 35 |
| D3 | Niacin deficiency | HIGH | Day 1–28 | Water niacin (see §4.0) |
| D4 | Coccidiosis | LOW–MODERATE | Day 11–21 | Single-week Amprolium course Week 2 |
| D5 | Newcastle Disease | MODERATE | Anytime (mixed-species farms only) | Optional Lasota Day 21 |
| D6 | Aspergillosis | MODERATE | Brooding, wet season | Ventilation, dry litter (no medication) |
| D7 | Internal parasites | MODERATE | Week 4+ | Fenbendazole Day 28, Day 49 |

## 7. Traditional Remedies

All five entries are stored as rows in `traditional_remedies` (see `03_WATER_HEALTH.md` §3.2) with `species_applies = ['duck']` (or broader). All have **0-day withdrawal**.

### 7.1 Aloe Vera (Aloe barbadensis) — immune / digestive / heat stress
- 50 ml strained gel solution per litre of drinking water
- Duration: 3–5 days; frequency: monthly or during stress.

### 7.2 Neem (Azadirachta indica) — anti-parasitic / immune
- Boil 100 g leaves in 2 L water, 15 min. Add 100 ml concentrate per litre drinking water.
- Duration: 3 days; every 2–4 weeks.

### 7.3 Papaya seeds (Carica papaya) — natural dewormer
- Dried, ground: 1 tsp per 10 birds in feed. Or fresh-blended: 1 papaya per 20 birds in water.
- **Research:** Adu et al. (2009) and Ameen et al. (2010) demonstrate efficacy against *Ascaridia galli* in poultry.
- Duration: 3 days; monthly (alternate with Fenbendazole).

### 7.4 Garlic (Allium sativum) — antimicrobial / respiratory
- 5–6 crushed cloves per litre of water, steep 2–4 h. Powder: ½ tsp per gallon.
- Duration: 3–5 days; weekly during outbreaks, monthly for prevention.

### 7.5 Turmeric (Curcuma longa) — anti-inflammatory / liver
- Grate 50 g fresh, boil in 1 L water 10 min, add to drinking water. Powder: ¼ tsp per gallon.
- Duration: 5–7 days; during stress or recovery.

### 7.6 Selection guidance (encoded as `recommendation_rules` in `species_config.traditional_remedies_meta`)

| Situation | Default recommendation |
|---|---|
| Arrival stress | Aloe OR commercial anti-stress |
| Mild digestive upset | Aloe / Garlic |
| Severe diarrhoea | Pharmaceutical |
| Routine deworming | Alternate Papaya / Fenbendazole |
| Coccidiosis outbreak | Pharmaceutical (Amprolium) |
| Mild respiratory | Garlic first |
| Severe respiratory | Pharmaceutical |
| Pre-sale (withdrawal) | Traditional only |

## 8. Emergency Protocols

| Emergency | First action | Within 24 h |
|---|---|---|
| Suspected DVH (sudden death cluster Day 1–10) | Isolate, call vet | Vaccinate any unvaccinated, post-mortem |
| Suspected Duck Plague (green diarrhoea, swollen eyelids) | Isolate, call vet | Emergency vaccination of in-contact birds |
| Niacin-deficiency leg curl | Increase niacin to 3 tsp/gal × 7 days | Audit niacin task completion log |
| Aspergillosis (gasping in brooder) | Remove wet litter, ventilate | Reduce stocking, no medication available |
| Heat stress (Tmax ≥ 35 °C) | Aloe water + electrolytes | Misting, shade |

Emergency overrides are issued via `POST /batches/:id/health-tasks/emergency` (see `03_WATER_HEALTH.md` §5.4). Conflict checks (CONVENTIONS §2.2) still apply.

## 11. Configuration JSON

Two seed rows are inserted into `species_config` (one per `duck_type`). Schema lives in `lib/db/src/schema/species-config.ts`.

```ts
// lib/db/src/schema/species-config.ts (excerpt)
export const speciesConfig = pgTable('species_config', {
  id: text('id').primaryKey(),                       // uuidv7
  species: text('species').notNull(),                // 'duck'
  variant: text('variant'),                          // 'meat' | 'layer' | null
  default_lifecycle_weeks: integer('default_lifecycle_weeks').notNull(),
  min_lifecycle_weeks: integer('min_lifecycle_weeks').notNull(),
  max_lifecycle_weeks: integer('max_lifecycle_weeks').notNull(),
  egg_production: boolean('egg_production').notNull(),
  egg_production_start_week: integer('egg_production_start_week'),
  water_table: jsonb('water_table').$type<WaterTableEntry[]>().notNull(),
  medication_schedule: jsonb('medication_schedule').$type<MedScheduleEntry[]>().notNull(),
  vaccination_schedule: jsonb('vaccination_schedule').$type<VaccinationEntry[]>().notNull(),
  withdrawal_periods: jsonb('withdrawal_periods').notNull(),
  traditional_remedies_enabled: boolean('traditional_remedies_enabled').notNull(),
  traditional_remedies_meta: jsonb('traditional_remedies_meta'),
  cost_estimate_pesewas: jsonb('cost_estimate_pesewas'),
  created_at: timestamp('created_at', { withTimezone: true }).defaultNow().notNull(),
}, (t) => ({
  uxSpeciesVariant: uniqueIndex('ux_species_variant').on(t.species, t.variant),
}));
```
